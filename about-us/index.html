About Us

Dependable, reliable, versatile are all words synonymous with the service that you’ll experience from Nautica Shipping and Logistics Ltd. As shipping and logistics specialists, we cater to the requirements of both importers and exporters, providing a range of shipping services, from all-inclusive door-to-door shipping packages to consultancy advice.

We pride ourselves on tailoring every job to meet our customers’ requirements. With exceptional customer service and loyalty, our customers can trust that Nautica will get the job done right, first time.

We provide comprehensive expert shipping and logistics services for:

  • Vehicles and automotive parts
  • Agricultural equipment and machinery
  • Household goods and furniture
  • Construction and building materials
  • Food products (including dry, chilled and frozen goods)
  • Health and cosmetic products
  • Clothing and retail goods
  • And much more….
